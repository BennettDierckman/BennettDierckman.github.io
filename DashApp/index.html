Healthcare Made Simple.

Dash is a Patient-Centered Healthcare Delivery App that promotes a more engaged and informed Patient, a more effective and effecient healthcare provider workforce and a reduction in unnecessary expenses to Patients. Dash’s decisions are guided by the vision of ensuring BOTH Doctors and Patients are informed of and actively involved in their treatment plans. Dash ensures that appropriate tests and treatments are deliverd at the appropriate place and time, for an appropriate cost.

  • Case Study: Diabetes

    Like most diabetes Patients, Tom has multiple Doctors. Each Doctor has their own verson of Tom’s Electronic Medical Record (“EMR”), and most often send for their own individual lab tests.

    If the results of a lab test ordered by Tom’s Primary Care Physician could used by Tom’s other Doctors, Tom could save $150 dollars for each test he does not have to re-take. That seems trivial, however, if extrapolated across all diabetics living in the United States, the cost savings will exceed $4.5 billion annually.

    Number of American Diabetics: 30,300,000 (CDC)

    Average Lab Test = $150 (Costhelper)

    Total Saved with Dash

    $150 X 30,300,000 = $4,545,000,000
