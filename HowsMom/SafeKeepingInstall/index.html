Apple Installation Instructions

1

Step 1

In the Safari Browser, go to app.safekeepingapp.com. Once there, tap the ‘Share’ icon (box with up-arrow) in the bottom middle of the screen.

2

Step 2

Next, slide through the Menu’s row of Grey Icons above the ‘Cancel’ Button and tap the “Add to Home Screen” option.

3

Step 3

Lastly, name the shortcut ”SafeKeeping“ and tap the "Add" option in the screen’s upper right coner.

Android Installation Instructions

1

Step 1

In the Google Chrome Browser, go to app.safekeepingapp.com. Once there, tap the ‘More’ icon (three vertical dots) the top right of the screen.

2

Step 2

Next, slide through the drop-down Menu’s list of available options and tap the “Add to Home Screen” option.

3

Step 3

Lastly, name the shortcut ”SafeKeeping“ and tap the "Add" option in the screen’s bottom right coner.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SafeKeeping

SafeKeeping is a family engagement dashboard that allows you to monitor the health and wellness of your loved one

How do I register for the SafeKeeping App

To register for the SafeKeeping App, visit your facilitie’s admissions or social services where you will receive an SMS invite to be granted access and complete registration

Is SafeKeeping in the App Store

SafeKeeping is a web based application meaning you can access it via URL once enrolled, and can install it on any device

How do I get to my SafeKeeping Dashboard

Utilize any connected device by your preferred browser and going to app.safekeepingapp.com
