Unmatched Customer Data Privacy for Financial Services

  • ShieldIO Provides Secure Data Access for Increased Profitability

    Financial Industry transaction data is the new Oil providing sources of revenue & innovative competitive differentiators. In a time where data & content is more essential to business success data hacks are commonplace in the industry. FSI Technology is in a state of flux adopting changes like moving to the cloud, while dealing with competitive pressures and ever increasing regulatory compliance.

    The cyber security market is flooded with many methods for securing networks and applications and a variety of masking and encryption technologies today. SDaaS Platform is the only solution that actually encrypts and secures data with KeyStore-less encryption, while enabling searching & analytics on encrypted data without decrypting it.

  • Financial Industry Challenges

    PCI Compliance with Credit Card and Payment Details, need to be encrypted yet searchable, so as to be able to analyze data and provide a better service

    Mobile Apps mean that data is transferred through non-trusted networks and to unknown-endpoints opening up the non-encrypted data to phishing and other attacks

    Deliver new business models to monetize data which might include sensitive customer identifiable information and transaction details

    Need to move data and applications from on premise to the cloud

    Ever increasing financial industry data security regulations & compliance obligations

    New and create external attacks to get access to customer identify, credit and net worth

    Unlimited access by internal Privileged users to all data including sensitive and confidential information

    Internal employees and contracts malicious or accidental access

    Local and offshore developers/testers requiring full data access for accurate development and testing

  • How ShieldIO Helps

    Provide simple and secure access to customer data to approved users

    Hides PII and PCI data from all but the data owner (Customer)

    Enables analytics on encrypted data without unlocking the security

    Enables call center staff to help and support customers without seeing sensitive data
