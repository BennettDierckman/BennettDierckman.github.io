Healthcare Industry

  1. Resources

  3. Healthcare

Encrypt PHI, PII and all other sensitive data, on-premise and in the cloud, without disrupting your data access or patient confidentiality

  • Healthcare is in a transformative state. With data sources on the rise, giving providers the ability to mine information giving unparalleled benefits to their users. This data explosion comes at a cost. Increased regulatory compliance with PHI and GDPR regulations and more extensive data theft by hackers who also wish to profit from the valuable data.

    With KeyStore-less New Age Homomorphic encryption SDaaS Platform, healthcare providers can use their full data with sensitive patient data remaining encrypted to deliver higher quality care while reducing cost with confidence that patient data is protected and compliant with privacy regulations. Data is secured without disruption to healthcare providers ability to mine the data for information, enabling them to deliver better more advanced services to their patients.

  • Healthcare Industry Challenges

    Protecting patient information while allowing sharing and accessing data anywhere, anytime and with any device

    Drugs trial data needs to be anonymized and secured. While not impeding on the results of the trial

    Deliver new business mode ls to monetize data which might include sensitive patient information

    More agile and quicker time to market drives the need to move data and applications from on premise to the cloud

    Increased global personal health data security regulations & compliance obligations impacting an ever increasing local and off-shore work force

    New and create external attacks to access patient health details

    Unlimited access by internal Privileged users to all data including sensitive and confidential information

    Internal employees and contracts malicious or accidental access

    Local and offshore developers/testers requiring full data access for accurate development and testing

  • How ShieldIO Helps

    Comply with government and industry regulation and

    Reduce the risk of sensitive data loss or misuse internally

    Elevate Healthcare Breach Concerns at the Core by Securing Patient Health Information (PHI) on Premise, in the Cloud, or Hyper-cloud Environments While Opening the Encrypted Data for Use
