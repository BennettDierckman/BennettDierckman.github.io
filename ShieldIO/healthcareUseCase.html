Healthcare Use Case

  1. Resources
  2. Industries
  3. Healthcare

Secure use of valuable personal healthcare data (PHI) based on authority to realize, access and use the data.

Ex. A large US research hospital makes significant revenue from drug trials and medical research, as well as the normal treatment of patients. That revenue is significantly reduced because much of the personal patient and care data (PII and PHI) are sensitive and, with no way to screen the large volumes involved, cannot be utilized.

ShieldIO’s SDaaS© is able to:

Provide simple and rapid deployment of the service.

Encrypt all sensitive data (PHI, PII, HIPPA) at rest, in transit and in use.

Move the data to the cloud with secure transaction access from remote sites enabling storage cost savings and wide access from users distributed across the country;

Provide a voice or message-activated NLP Bot to very easily search the data with no training based on questions expressed in natural conversational language;

Enable unstructured information, such as reports, documents and emails with sensitive information, to be redacted based on any search pattern;

Search the data at very high volume in a way that current and historical data can be included in analysis and research;

Enable access to structured patient data in a tiered model based on the user’s authority level to see sensitive information; Medical Consultants can see clinical information; drug companies can see trial information; patients/doctors can see appropriate personal information. All other sensitive information is simply not presented to any other user.

This service is any platform in market today. While this use case is being deployed in a healthcare environment, the security and accessibility of personal sensitive information has application in Government, Law Enforcement, Telecommunications, Online Media and Financial Services segments in the same manner.
