Insurance Providers

Encrypt all data enabling a fully secured company to customer transaction. While allowing for innovation and monetization of all the data including secured fields

  • Insurance companies have used different technologies and database to better understand their customers and changing regional and geo market needs. As a result, they might have a mix of new as well as out dated mix-and-match security solutions. In an environment with ever increasing value of insurance providers data, the cyber criminals are using most advanced cyber weapons available to get access to the valuable data.

    With KeyStore-less Homomorphic encryption SDaaS Platform insurance providers can use their full data with sensitive patient or financial data remaining encrypted to deliver higher quality care while reducing cost with confidence that patient data is protected and compliant with privacy regulations.

    SDaaS provides a platform which secures data at rest, in-transit and in memory protecting diverse data requirements of insurance companies delivering a true security from breach.

    Improve your business fundamentals by changing how you manage risk

    The ability to create the customer intimacy required to cross-sell and upsell

    Remove business automation by brining data from diverse sources together while complying with compliance and regulations.

    Safely utilize data which includes customer health and financial data making transformative business changes to retain competitive advantage.
