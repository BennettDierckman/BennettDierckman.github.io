Narrtive

Go Outside And Live (goal) is a nonprofit agency that works with Parks and Recreation professionals and help them organize, plan, and promote outdoor activities in Parks throughout the Midwest. This I.T. Project Management Plan will explain how GOAL plans to use these funds to help modernize its I.T. infrastructure. GOAL will use the most of the initial $125,000 for one-time costs. Some of these costs will include new PC's and overhead projectors for two new computer labs, B&W and Color heavy duty laser printers that will be used by staff for the production of training/promotional material, and tablet computers that will be used by field instructors. Goal will use the additional $50,000 for re-occurring costs such as internet service, web server, and Local Area Network-LAN both wired and wireless.
