What's a Hoosier Helper Showcase?


The "Hoosier Helper Showcase" is a Zip file that can be downloaded by students, addapted to display the students bio and projects that the student will upload to winSCP and then view on the internet. Their Hoosier Helper Showcase will be used as a refrance during their everyday studies. Students will be able to upload files containing their own projects and personal work that they would like for friends family and possibly even potential employers to look at.


The Zip file will contain html files titled homeTemplate.html, projectsTemplate.html, toolsTemplate.html, and referancesTemplate.html. Each of these will be a webpage on the students Hoosier Helper Showcase. Most importantly the Zip file will contain instructions.txt which is a word document that the student will use to learn how to use notepad++, help them follow the in-code comments that will tell them where to put content, and show them how to upload files to winSCP.
