What are Primary Keys?

Primary Keys are a unique value that represents a whole row, the rows will be diferentated by the primary key.

What are Foriegn Keys?

Foriegn Keys, are keys that exisist in one table but refer to the primary key of another, alowing us to be able to link the tables together.

What is the difference between a tuple and an attribute?

Attributes act as column headings in the relational table, and describes the data contained in each column, where a Tuple, refers to an entire row of the table.

What is the Scheme of the courses table?

The Scheme of the courses table contains the Forigen key "ClassID", name, course number, and number of credit hours.
