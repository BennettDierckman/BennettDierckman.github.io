Dan Wright

Principal Consultant

Dan has a B.S. in Computer Science from Miami University and is passionate about jealously serving his customer. He lives by the adage:

You gain trust in inches and lose it in miles …

Dan has spent the last 30 years working for several startups helping successfully launch new products and services in healthcare, aviation and financial industries. Dan created a custom content management system for jet engine maintenance based on a mini-computer network and optical storage for GE Aviation that was later sold to an industry vendor.

A startup, MedPlus, hired Dan to create an electronic medical records solution for healthcare holding the position as VP of Product Development and Operations. Dan managed a team of over 35 people and was responsible for the solution design, software development, sales, implementation and support for both the ChartMaxx and OptiMaxx product lines. During his 10 year tenure, Dan’s team sold and implemented over 150 customers. Dan helped take MedPlus public in 1995 and was acquired in 2002 by Dan’s largest customer, Quest Diagnostics.

Following the sale of MedPlus Dan joined FileNet as a Sales Engineer which was later acquired by IBM before leaving for another startup Viewpointe LLC.

Dan specialized in Business Process Management, Imaging, Information Governance, Records Management and Case Management. Dan has extensive experience with the Software Development Life Cycle/Quality Control, taught Project Management at GE Aviation and held a Six Sigma black belt. Dan has 5+ years’ experience with cloud migrations, cyber security and governance in both the Healthcare and the Finance industries.
